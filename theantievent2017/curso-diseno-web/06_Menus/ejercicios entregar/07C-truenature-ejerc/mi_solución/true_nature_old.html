WELCOME TO TRUENATURE

Posted by Someone on February 10, 2011 Comments (64) Full article
True Nature Unfolds is the first true full-length album by the Finnish post-metal band Callisto. It was first released in 2004 by Fullsteam Records, and then re-released in 2005 by Earache Records with different artwork. The lyrics reference Christian themes in most of the songs.

TRACK TITLE

Posted by Someone on February 10, 2011 Comments (64) Full article
The introductory track's title is that of a geographic coordinate that is pointing to Golgotha, a hill just outside the walls of ancient Jerusalem where Jesus Christ was crucified according to the Bible.

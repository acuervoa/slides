Love's not time's fool, though rosy lips and cheeks whose worth's unknown.

Although his height be taken. That looks on tempests and is never shaken; within his bending sickle's compass come; if this be error and upon me proved. Which alters when it alteration finds, love alters not with his brief hours and weeks, oh, no, it is an ever fixed mark.
